About Our Club

The University of St. Thomas Management Consulting Club is a student organization dedicated to developing the next generation of strategic consultants. We provide hands-on experience through real client projects, case competitions, and professional development opportunities.

Client Projects

Work with real organizations to solve complex business challenges

Case Competitions

Compete in regional and national case study competitions

Professional Development

Build consulting skills through workshops and industry connections

Networking Events

Connect with consulting professionals, alumni, and industry leaders
