About

Jen Kalla serves as the Faculty Advisor for the UST Management Consulting Club, providing invaluable guidance and industry insights to support student development. With extensive experience in business and consulting, Jen brings real-world perspective to the club's activities and helps bridge the gap between academic learning and professional practice.

Her mentorship and connections to the consulting industry have been instrumental in creating meaningful opportunities for club members and ensuring the organization remains aligned with industry standards and expectations.

Role & Impact

As Faculty Advisor, Jen provides strategic counsel to club leadership, facilitates connections with industry professionals, and ensures that the club's programs align with both academic excellence and professional development goals. She serves as a mentor, resource, and advocate for students pursuing careers in management consulting.

Focus Areas

  • Strategic Guidance & Mentorship
  • Industry Connections & Networking
  • Program Development & Quality Assurance
  • Career Development Support
  • Academic-Professional Integration
