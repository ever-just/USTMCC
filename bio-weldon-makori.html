About

Weldon Makori serves as Vice President of the UST Management Consulting Club, playing a crucial role in supporting club operations and driving member engagement initiatives. With a keen focus on operational excellence and community building, Weldon ensures that the club runs smoothly and that every member has meaningful opportunities to grow and succeed.

His dedication to member experience and program development has been instrumental in creating an inclusive, dynamic environment where students can thrive and prepare for careers in consulting.

Responsibilities

As Vice President, Weldon oversees day-to-day operations, coordinates events and workshops, and serves as a key liaison between club leadership and members. He works closely with the President to execute the club's strategic vision while ensuring that member feedback is heard and acted upon.

Focus Areas

  • Club Operations & Event Coordination
  • Member Engagement & Experience
  • Workshop & Training Program Development
  • Internal Communications
  • Community Building Initiatives

Get in Touch

Have questions about club events or want to get more involved? Connect with Weldon on LinkedIn or reach out through the club's contact form.
