About

Andrew Zimmerman serves as Treasurer of the UST Management Consulting Club, where he manages the club's finances and ensures sustainable growth. With a strong analytical mindset and attention to detail, Andrew oversees budgeting, financial planning, and resource allocation to support the club's ambitious programs and initiatives.

His financial stewardship enables the club to invest in high-quality events, partnerships, and member development opportunities while maintaining fiscal responsibility.

Responsibilities

As Treasurer, Andrew manages all financial aspects of the club, including budget planning, expense tracking, fundraising coordination, and financial reporting. He works closely with leadership to ensure that financial resources are allocated strategically to maximize member value and club impact.

Focus Areas

  • Financial Planning & Budget Management
  • Expense Tracking & Reporting
  • Fundraising & Sponsorship Coordination
  • Resource Allocation & Optimization
  • Financial Sustainability Planning

Get in Touch

Interested in sponsorship opportunities or have questions about club finances? Connect with Andrew on LinkedIn or reach out through the club's contact form.
